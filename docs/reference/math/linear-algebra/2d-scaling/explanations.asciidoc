= 2D Scaling

Scaling a point with <</reference/math/coordinate-systems/homogeneous-coordinates/explanations#,homogeneous coordinates>> stem:[(x, y, w)] by a factor stem:[s_\mathrm{x}] along the X-axis and a factor stem:[s_\mathrm{y}] along the Z-axis
is achieved by the following matrix multiplication:

[stem]
++++
  \begin{bmatrix}
    s_\mathrm{x} & 0 & 0 \\
    0 & s_\mathrm{y} & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 1 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  \cdot
  \begin{bmatrix}
    x \\
    y \\
    w \\
  \end{bmatrix}
++++

[NOTE,caption="Example"]
====
Scaling stem:[P(3, 5)] by 2 along the X-axis:

[stem]
++++
  \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 0 & 0 \\
    0 & 1 & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 1 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  \cdot
  \begin{bmatrix}
    3 \\
    5 \\
    1 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  =
  \begin{bmatrix}
    6 \\
    10 \\
    1 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
++++
====
