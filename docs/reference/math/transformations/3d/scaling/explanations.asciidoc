= 3D Scaling

Scaling a point with <</reference/math/homogeneous-coordinates/explanations#,homogeneous coordinates>> stem:[(x, y, z, w)] by factors stem:[s_\mathrm{x}], stem:[s_\mathrm{y}] and stem:[s_\mathrm{z}] along the X-, Y- and Z-axis,
respectively, is achieved by the following matrix multiplication:

[stem]
++++
  \begin{bmatrix}
    s_\mathrm{x} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
    0 & s_\mathrm{y} & 0 & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & s_\mathrm{z} & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  \cdot
  \begin{bmatrix}
    x \\
    y \\
    z \\
    w \\
  \end{bmatrix}
++++
