= 3D Translation

Translating a point with <</reference/math/coordinate-systems/homogeneous-coordinates/explanations#,homogeneous coordinates>> stem:[(x, y, z, w)] by a vector stem:[(\Delta x, \Delta y, \Delta z, 0)]
can be computed using the following matrix multiplication:

[stem]
++++
  \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 0 & 0 & \Delta x \\
    0 & 1 & 0 & \Delta y \\
    0 & 0 & 1 & \Delta z \\
    0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  \cdot
  \begin{bmatrix}
    x \\
    y \\
    z \\
    w \\
  \end{bmatrix}
++++

Note that translation only has any effect on points (stem:[w = 1]): vectors (stem:[w = 0]) remain unaffected.
