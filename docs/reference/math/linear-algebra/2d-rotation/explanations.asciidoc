= 2D Rotation

Rotating a point with <</reference/math/coordinate-systems/homogeneous-coordinates/explanations#,homogeneous coordinates>> stem:[(x, y, w)] around the origin by an angle stem:[\alpha] can be achieved using

[stem]
++++
  \begin{bmatrix}
    \cos\alpha & -\sin\alpha & 0 \\
    \sin\alpha & \cos\alpha & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 1 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  \cdot
  \begin{bmatrix}
    x \\
    y \\
    w \\
  \end{bmatrix}
++++
